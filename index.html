Kavi Harshawat

Kavi is a user experience researcher and designer based in San Francisco, CA. He is a fan of movies, zombies, and the oxford comma.

Work

Kavi was a 2014 Code for America Fellow where he worked with the city of Denver to help improve the way they built services for residents using technology. He worked on improving civic engagement and bring better user experiences to government through his project GoodCTZN. Previously, Kavi worked at Aardvark and Google, as an early member of the Google+ team.
